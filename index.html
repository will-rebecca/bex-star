Sociological Research

The Relation Between Socioeconomic Status & Trauma
Trauma is a deeply distressing and or disruptive experience that can have lasting effects that negatively impact a person’s well-being. While many students encounter difficulties during the course of their academic adventure, those from lower-income households may have been disproportionately exposed to traumatic experiences both before and also while they attended college.
