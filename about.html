DCI & WGI

Together, WGI and DCI represent the highest level of pageantry arts, blending athleticism, creativity, and passion into unforgettable performances.

DCI is a summer league that showcases elite marching ensembles–often featuring some of the most skilled color guard performers in the world. Color guard, which originated as a visual extension of margin bands, has evolved into its own art form both in WGI’s indoor season and indoor marching activities like DCI.

My Journey in Color Guard

I began color guard in high school, where I fell in love with the art form and eventually became captain during my senior year. That leadership experience sparked something in me, and on a whim, I auditioned for the Troopers Drum and Bugle Corps–and earned a spot. That spontaneous decision led to three unforgettable summers performing with some of the most passionate and dedicated people I’ve ever met.

Those experiences shaped my resilience, deepened my love for performance, and taught me the value of showing up fully no matter the conditions.
